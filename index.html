Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes across various industries, from small startups to large enterprises. Our services are particularly well-suited for SMEs in South Africa looking to enhance their IT infrastructure, as well as companies requiring specialized Linux server management or custom software development.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A standard website might take 4-6 weeks, while custom software development or cloud migration projects can take 2-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your project requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages tailored to your needs. This includes system monitoring, security updates, bug fixes, and technical support. We believe in long-term partnerships and are committed to your continued success.

What makes your Linux server management different?

Our team consists of certified Linux engineers with enterprise-level experience across Ubuntu, SUSE, and RHEL systems. We focus on security hardening, performance optimization, and proactive monitoring. Unlike generic IT support, we specialize in Linux environments and understand the unique requirements of enterprise server infrastructure.

Can you work with our existing IT infrastructure?

Yes! We specialize in integrating with existing systems. Whether you need to upgrade current infrastructure, migrate to the cloud, or add new capabilities, we ensure seamless integration without disrupting your operations. We conduct thorough assessments and create migration plans that minimize risk.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment structures to accommodate different business needs. Typically, we require a deposit to begin work, with milestone-based payments throughout the project. For ongoing services like support and maintenance, we offer monthly subscription plans. We'll discuss the best payment arrangement during our initial consultation.

Do you provide training for our staff?

Yes! We include comprehensive training and documentation with every project. We ensure your team is fully equipped to use and maintain the solutions we implement. Training can be conducted on-site or remotely, depending on your preferences.

Are you available for emergency support?

For clients with support contracts, we offer priority emergency support with rapid response times. We understand that IT issues can't wait, and we're committed to minimizing downtime for your business. Contact us to discuss emergency support options for your specific needs.
